Welcome to the study

Instructions

In this study, you will be presented with a series of first-person images. You may or may not be presented with a list of activities or objects to choose from on the right hand side of the page. Your task is to choose the best description of activities being performed by the camera wearer in the image. If the presented list of activities or objects does not contain the activity or object you want to describe, please use the "other" text box to type in your answer.

Below are some examples snippets of annotations performed.

Pick the most appropriate activity in the list if you can find it, select it and click submit


If you are not satisfied by the provided options, type in your own answer on the other text box

If the activity being performed in the first-person image is not clear, make your best guess

Frequently Asked Questions

What if the activity is not clear in the picture?
We ask you to choose the best description of the activity being performed in the image. If you are not sure, please put your best guess in the text box or choose the best answer from the suggestion list on the right side of the page.

Some of the suggestions provided are nouns or names of objects rather than activities. Do I select nouns?
We ask you to choose the best description of the activity being performed in the image. If the suggested list are nouns, please do your best to find an activity that represents the image or type in the text box to write the activity.
